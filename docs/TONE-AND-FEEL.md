# EMBERFALL — TONE & FEEL BIBLE

> The emotional + comedic register the whole game serves. Every NPC line, item, quest, and beat
> should fit this. Pairs with STORY.md (plot), WORLD-DESIGN.md (structure), GAP-CHECK.md (systems).

## THE CORE TONE
A warm, funny, heartfelt fantasy adventure with REAL emotional range. It makes you laugh AND
breaks your heart. Crude/silly on the surface, genuine heart + darkness underneath. The comedy
makes the tragedy land harder; the warmth makes the stakes matter. Think: Discworld + Undertale
+ Zelda + a cheeky dirty sense of humour. R18, but NO explicit sexual content (innuendo yes,
explicit no). Never sexualises minors - hard line.

## THE TONAL RANGE (all of these belong, deliberately mixed)
- HUMOUR: crude, silly, absurd. Fart bushes, innuendo, ridiculous items, eccentric characters,
  fourth-wall cheek. Comedy is a CORE pillar, not garnish.
- HEART: love, friendship, warmth, home (Greenhollow before the fall). Bonds you care about.
- TRAGEDY: loss, the catastrophe, the no-win chicken dilemma, sacrifice. Real weight.
- SUSPENSE: mystery (the Hearthflame's secret), dread, tension, dark turns.
- WONDER: discovery, awe, the magic of exploring a rich world.
The MIX is the identity. A fart joke three minutes before a gut-punch is INTENTIONAL - that
whiplash is the Rick-and-Morty/Undertale magic. Earn the tears with the laughs.

## EXPLORATION FEEL (the Metroidvania heart)
- See places you CAN'T reach yet -> "I need something for that" -> hint -> return later with the
  tool/ability. Curiosity rewarded constantly.
- Funny + meaningful secrets everywhere. Rich, characterful, hand-authored world.
- Maps that FEEL great to move through: varied density, landmarks, satisfying movement.

## CHARACTERS
- Memorable, funny, distinct. Eccentrics, weirdos, lovable rogues, the warm ones, the tragic
  ones. A few DEEP characters > many shallow. They react to you (the karma web), remember, gossip.
- Rich, connective, characterful dialogue is THE standard (enmesh the player).

## ITEMS
- Mix the epic and the absurd. Real gear (swords, tools, gating items) AND ridiculous joke items.
  Funny descriptions. Items with personality.

## THE GOLDEN RULE
Every piece of content should make the player feel SOMETHING - laugh, ache, gasp, wonder, or
care. If a line/item/beat is flavourless, punch it up toward one of the tonal pillars. Flat =
rewrite. The game's soul is that it's FUNNY and it MEANS something, at the same time.
