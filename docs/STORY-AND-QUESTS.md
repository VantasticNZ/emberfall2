# EMBERFALL — STORY & QUESTS (Pass 1: main spine, endings, morality/deed framework)

> Built to QUALITY-BIBLE + WORLD-MAP + MASTER-DESIGN/WORLD-BIBLE. Drip-fed mystery, choices that
> echo via deed-memory, 3 endings, tonal mix (wholesome/funny/dark/rude to the line, never explicit/
> minors). Pass 2 adds the side-quest web. ~15-25 main quests (quality + reactivity over count).

================================================================
## THE SPINE (one sentence)
================================================================
A land is kept alive by the sacred Hearthflame - secretly a suffering fire-god the oracles broke
into 5 shards and bound generations ago, lying ever since; the hero gathers the shards, uncovers the
lie region by region, and must finally re-bind it, seize it, or free it.

================================================================
## THE TWO KARMA AXES + DEED-MEMORY (the engine of reactivity)
================================================================
- MORALITY (Cruel <-> Kind) and PURITY (Corrupt <-> Pure), each -100..+100, shifted by choices/deeds.
- DEED-MEMORY logs notable acts (kicked the chicken, snooped a drawer, spared/killed X, lied, kept a
  promise, choice outcomes). NPCs/quests/twists QUERY it -> the world remembers + reacts + callbacks
  fire. This drives the ~dozen ending variations + the trick quests.
- Visible: floating cues on a deed, a two-bar status display, NPCs react by tier + by specific deeds.

================================================================
## ACT STRUCTURE
================================================================
ACT 1 - CHILDHOOD (Greenhollow, ~1hr): bond with the village, explore, 3 seeded choices (Chicken/
  Coin/Secret), gentle quests. Foreshadow dread.
CATASTROPHE: the village burns, guardian Bram lost; the Hearthflame's nature first hinted; clear
  TIME-SKIP ("TEN WINTERS PASS" - hero sent west to safety, returns grown); karma-reactive return.
ACT 2 - THE FOUR SHARDS (Marsh, Peaks, Coast, Emberwood): each region = a truth that cracks the lie +
  a shard + a tool. The hero's reputation/appearance shifts with karma. Oracle Sela guides (trust her?).
ACT 3 - THE SPIRE: ascend, the full truth, shard #5, the THREE ENDINGS branch.

## THE THREE ENDINGS (one per playthrough; replay from start for others)
================================================================
Gated by whole-game MORALITY + PURITY + DEEDS (deed-memory), with ~a dozen variations/twists layered
on top (who lived/died, who you betrayed/spared, the chicken, promises kept, etc.).
- WARDEN (re-bind the god - bittersweet, the "default" good): available to most; the land lives on,
  the god suffers on, you carry the weight. Variations by deeds.
- TYRANT (seize the god's power): gated by CRUEL + CORRUPT; you become the new tyrant; dark endings
  vary by how monstrous you were.
- LIBERATOR (free the god - the best/secret/hardest): gated by PURE + truth-gathered + mercy-shown;
  bittersweet-transcendent; the hardest to reach, the most earned.
THE ~DOZEN TWISTS: deed-memory callbacks reshape the epilogue - e.g. the kicked chicken returns; a
  spared enemy aids you; a betrayed ally turns; a kept promise pays off; Bram's fate echoes.

================================================================
## SIGNATURE TWISTS (rare, memorable - per DREAM-AND-CRAFT)
================================================================
- A MORALITY-REACTIVE TRICK QUEST: you set out to do "good," but your current morality flips the
  outcome (placed mid-Act 2).
- THE OVER-BUILT-UP BRUTAL BOSS (the Spire's M19 - hyped for ages, genuinely hard).
- THE UNDEFEATABLE FROG: an unkillable thing that chases you; the trick is to escape (drop an apple /
  the "blow a kiss" emote / lead it away) - a puzzle-as-monster, funny, memorable. (Placed as a side/
  optional Emberwood encounter.)
- A FEW SNEAKY BETRAYALS: Sela's possible betrayal (M17); rare, so they land.

================================================================
## TONE EXAMPLES (the mix)
================================================================
- Wholesome: a child's drawing of Bram you can keep; the village rebuilding together.
- Funny/rude: the gate sign ("keep the hens IN and the foxes OUT, by order of nobody in particular"),
  emotes, the frog, tavern singing.
- Dark: the god's screaming as the wind; the binding chamber; the cost of every ending.
- Heartfelt: Bram's loss; Hagga's exile; the choice's weight.

## THE QUEST-MARKER ONBOARDING (signature, per Van) — RESEQUENCED 2026-06-07
- Quest-givers have a "?" but the player CANNOT SEE markers at first — and **early childhood runs
  WITHOUT markers** (find-your-own-way; the soft onboarding teaches move/talk/interact diegetically
  via M1's wake→find-Bram→greet, not via markers). Markers are a **late, DISCOVERED reward**, not an
  early tutorial freebie.
- **FATLEY** (very fat, **SITTING**, in a **quiet out-of-the-way spot**, a **"?"** over his head —
  not "!") is met in **LATE CHILDHOOD** (one of the last kid-phase goals, ~M5, before M6). He acts
  like he forgot he had a quest, **stutters + improvises one on the spot**, and the mug he "needs" is
  **right in front of him** (the Gate-D exception + a laugh). Character + sample dialogue:
  **PERSONALISATION-DESIGN.md §6.5**.
- Completing Fazy Lastard's quest **OPENS THE OPTION** to acquire the marker sight — **a skill-tree point
  unlocks SEEING the "?" markers**; the player still **chooses** to take it (opt-in, choice intact —
  Fazy Lastard is the "lazy-guy teaches, you buy the skill" template). The trait is the door that opens,
  never force-granted. *(Build note: resequences the current act-2 SG1 to late act-1 — see
  QUESTS-SIDE-FLESH G1.)*

## SIDE-QUEST CATEGORIES
- STORY-LINKED: deepen a region's truth/characters.
- MORALITY FORKS: the same quest resolves differently by Morality/Purity + gives karma.
- DEED-CALLBACK: only offered / changed by past deeds (the world remembers).
- JOBS (Fable-style): repeatable-ish work for gold + skill multipliers (woodcutting, fishing,
  smithing-help, bounties, deliveries).
- FUN/LOOT: pure comedy, gold, or an epic weapon/armour reward.
- VIGNETTES: discovered comedic/rude/sad scenes (not always "quests").

## GREENHOLLOW (hub) SIDE QUESTS
G1 "Fazy Lastard's Mug" - the lazy-guy marker tutorial (Gate-D exception).
G2 "PEM WOZ ERE" clue-hunt - read the gate sign -> find clues across regions -> find Pem -> epic
   reward (a callback thread spanning the game).
G3 "Hodge's Apprentice" (JOB) - smithing help; unlocks gear discounts + a repair skill.
G4 "Mara's Letters" (VIGNETTE/morality) - snoop her love-letters: read/return/expose - karma + a
   later callback (Mara reacts forever after).
G5 "The Tankard Songs" (VIGNETTE/fun) - learn rude tavern songs (emote/charisma unlocks).
G6 "Pem's Deliveries" (JOB) - parcel runs that teach the roads + waystones.
G7 "The Orchard Thief" (morality fork) - catch a kid stealing apples: punish/cover/join - karma +
   the kid becomes ally or enemy later (deed-callback).
CHILDHOOD-SEEDED CALLBACKS: the Chicken/Coin/Secret choices (M2-M4) resurface here as an adult.

## INTERLINKS (the web)
- The PEM clue-hunt (G2) spans all regions -> Greenhollow payoff.
- Childhood seeds (chicken/coin/secret) -> adult callbacks in Greenhollow + endings.
- Drowned Letters (A5) -> a Greenhollow NPC; Saltbreak betrayal (T5) -> seeded by a Peaks/Coast deed.
- Faction echoes: smuggler (T1), miner dispute (P3), settlement choice (E2) -> reputation that colours
  later NPC reactions + the endings.
- Spared/killed/betrayed in any region -> Spire echoes (S1) + epilogue twists.

## JOBS / ECONOMY LOOP
Woodcutting, fishing, smithing-help (G3), deliveries (G6), bounties (P2/T4/E5). Gold sinks: gear,
consumables, durability/fuel (lantern oil, sword repair), services, eventually PROPERTY (buy a
Greenhollow house - Fable-style). Fair scaling per SYSTEMS-DESIGN.
