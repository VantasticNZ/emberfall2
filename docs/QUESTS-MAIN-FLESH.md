# EMBERFALL — MAIN QUESTS, FULLY FLESHED (to QUALITY-BIBLE Gate D/H)

> Each quest: GOAL, beats/steps (spread out, no item-in-front), key dialogue beats, CHOICES + karma/
> deed hooks, rewards, links. Tone = the mix. Pass: Act 1 (M1-M6). Acts 2-3 follow.

================================================================
# ACT 1 — CHILDHOOD (Greenhollow, ~1hr) + CATASTROPHE
================================================================

## M1 — "A Greenhollow Morning" (tutorial)
GOAL: wake as a child; learn move/talk/interact; meet the village; feel it's alive.
BEATS: wake in your home (Mara, your guardian-figure, calls you down) -> walk the village -> guardian
BRAM (the warm blacksmith-protector) greets you at the forge -> simple "say hello to 3 villagers"
(teaches talk + gestures) -> Bram gives you a child's wooden toy (not a weapon).
DIALOGUE BEAT: Bram - warm, gruff, funny ("Mind the hens, and mind Old Edda — she bites worse'n the
hens."). Establishes love so the loss hurts.
CHOICE: none (onboarding). DEED: greeting style (wave vs ignore) seeds early Morality nudge.
REWARD: free roam unlocked, toy keepsake (callback item).
LINKS: sets up Bram (M6 loss), Mara (G4), the village.

## M2 — "Chores + Mischief" (SEEDED CHOICE 1: the Chicken)
GOAL: gentle spread-out chores; meet the chicken; deed-memory intro.
BEATS: Mara asks help with 3 chores across the MEADOW (travel, not in-front): fetch eggs (far coop),
water the orchard (brook), shoo a hen back. The hen FLEES + leads a short chase (not in front of the
giver). A kid, TAM, dares you about the hen.
CHOICE 1 (the Chicken): help catch it gently (KIND) / kick it (CRUEL — logged forever, callbacks in
adult game + ending) / free it from the coop entirely (CHAOTIC, minor Purity).
DIALOGUE: Tam - cheeky kid ("Bet you won't boot it. Bet you a sweet."). 
REWARD: a few coins, Mara's thanks. DEED: chicken choice logged (signature callback).
LINKS: the kicked chicken returns (Greenhollow adult + endings).

## M3 — "The Coin" (SEEDED CHOICE 2)
GOAL: a found coin tests honesty.
BEATS: find a dropped coin-purse near the market -> the owner (a flustered merchant, e.g. PHIL
McCRACKEN) is searching nearby.
CHOICE 2: return it (KIND/PURE) / keep it quietly (CRUEL-lite/CORRUPT-lite) / use it to buy a friend
a treat (KIND, social rep).
DIALOGUE: McCracken - fretful, funny relief if returned. REWARD: varies by choice (gratitude+small
gift / the coins / a friend's loyalty). DEED logged.
LINKS: merchant remembers you (adult shop prices/attitude).

## M4 — "The Boarded Cave" (SEEDED CHOICE 3: the Secret)
GOAL: a forbidden boarded cave at the meadow edge; curiosity vs rules.
BEATS: kids whisper about the cave -> you find it boarded -> a loose plank.
CHOICE 3 (the Secret): tell an adult (PURE/cautious — Bram warns you off, warmth) / explore alone
(brave/reckless — find a small treasure + a faint unsettling carving hinting at the Ember) / dare a
friend to go (CHAOTIC — consequence if they get hurt, deed-logged).
DIALOGUE: ominous carving - first faint seed of the god ("a flame that weeps"). 
REWARD: minor treasure / lore seed / social outcome. DEED logged.
LINKS: the carving foreshadows the truth; cave revisited as adult.

## M5 — "The Festival" (build-up + dread)
GOAL: the Hearthflame Festival — warmth, then wrongness. Pace the dread.
BEATS: help prepare (hang lanterns, gather wood — light tasks) -> the festival: music, the Tankard
songs (G5 tie), villagers happy -> the Oracle blesses the Hearthflame -> the flame FLICKERS WRONG;
Bram uneasy ("Flame's never done that."); a cold wind; an oracle's too-quick reassurance.
DIALOGUE: warmth turning to unease; Bram's protective instinct. The first real DREAD.
CHOICE: small — comfort a scared child (KIND) or not. DEED logged.
REWARD: festival keepsake; emotional peak before the fall.
LINKS: directly into M6; the "wrong flame" pays off as the god's sickness.

## M6 — "The Night It Burned" (CATASTROPHE)
GOAL: the village burns; Bram lost; the flame's nature hinted; the time-skip; karma-reactive return
setup.
BEATS: night — the Hearthflame ERUPTS/goes wrong -> fire spreads -> you flee with Bram -> Bram gets
you out but is LOST to the flame (heartfelt gut-punch; he gives you the toy back / a last word) ->
an Oracle (SELA) finds you in the ash -> EXPLANATION beat: the flame is more than fire, it must be
tended, you are sent WEST to safety -> "TEN WINTERS PASS" (clear time-skip card) -> you are now an
adult, returning. The return is KARMA-REACTIVE (kind childhood = villagers warm/hopeful; cruel = wary/
the kicked chicken's owner remembers).
DIALOGUE: Bram's loss (earn the tears); Sela - composed, kind, slightly too composed (seed of doubt).
CHOICE: a grief beat — what you say over the ash (shapes early adult tone). DEED logged.
REWARD: transition to Act 2; the central wound that drives everything.
LINKS: Bram's loss echoes to the endings; Sela's framing seeds her M17 betrayal possibility; the
"sent west / ten winters" answers Van's "where did I go / why changed" note.

# ACT 2 — RETURN + THE FOUR SHARDS (M7-M17)
================================================================

## M7 — "Ten Winters Gone" (return; earn first weapon)
GOAL: return to Greenhollow as adult; the world reacts to childhood karma; Sela sets the journey;
earn your first real weapon.
BEATS: arrive at a rebuilt-but-scarred Greenhollow -> KARMA-REACTIVE welcome (kind=warm, hopeful;
cruel=wary; specific deed callbacks: kicked-chicken owner cold, returned-coin merchant friendly) ->
Sela explains the Hearthflame is failing across the land (the sickness/wind), the 5 shards must be
recovered, sends you WEST to the Marsh -> HODGE quest: bring materials, he forges your first blade
(cut a branch via bashing a tree -> wooden sword -> later steel). Meet adult cast + FATLEY (G1 marker
tutorial available).
CHOICE: accept Sela's framing at face value or press her (seeds doubt; WIS/dialogue). DEED logged.
REWARD: first weapon; Greenhollow hub unlocked; west road opens.
LINKS: Sela (M10/M17), Hodge (G3), the marker system (G1).

## M8 — "Into the Ashen Marsh"
GOAL: reach Mirefen; meet Hagga.
BEATS: travel west (forest bleeds to bog) -> Mirefen settlement on stilts; bog-folk wary (Elder Yssa)
-> Yssa warns of Hagga across the black water ("the oracles exiled her — ask why") -> reach Hagga's
hut.
DIALOGUE: Yssa - superstitious, weary; Hagga - sharp, sad, truth-telling exile.
CHOICE: how you treat the wary bog-folk (KIND/CRUEL). DEED logged. REWARD: Marsh hub, Hagga met.
LINKS: A1/A2 side quests; sets up M9-M10.

## M9 — "The Sunken Shrine" (LANTERN tool; SHARD #1; boss)
GOAL: dungeon; earn the lantern; first shard.
BEATS: Hagga points to the shrine -> enter the dark dungeon -> find the LANTERN (lights dark, reveals
paths, gates) -> dark/light puzzles -> DROWNED GUARDIAN boss (learn-the-pattern: lantern-light strips
its shroud, then strike; dodge lunge, block volley; 3-5 light+hit cycles) -> claim SHARD #1.
REWARD: lantern (tool), shard #1, heart container. DEED: none major.
LINKS: lantern opens prior dark spots (backtrack); shard count drives Act 2.

## M10 — "Hagga's Truth" (first lie cracked; CHOICE echoes)
GOAL: the first revelation + a choice that echoes.
BEATS: return to Hagga with the shard -> she reveals: the Hearthflame is a LIVING GOD, broken + bound
by the oracles, lying for generations; its dying IS the sickness.
CHOICE (big, echoes): BELIEVE Hagga (PURE/truth-path; she becomes an ally) / REPORT her to Sela
(loyal-to-oracles; Sela "handles" Hagga — dark, CORRUPT nudge; Hagga gone) / KEEP SILENT (cautious;
keep both options open).
DIALOGUE: Hagga's "the Hearthflame was never just a fire, child... its screaming is the wind off the
mountain." REWARD: truth (lore), ally/enemy state set. DEED logged (major — affects M17 + endings).
LINKS: the spine's core reveal; Sela's reaction seeds M17; ending-gating begins.

## M11 — "The Sundered Peaks"
GOAL: reach the mountains; the oracle order's old seat.
BEATS: north road (grassland rises to foothills) -> a terraced stone town; hardy folk -> rumours the
old oracle ORDER ruled from Cinder Keep -> the way up needs a tool you don't have yet (gated) -> find
the route via a local (P3 miner tie).
CHOICE: side in a local dispute (P3 hook). DEED logged. REWARD: Peaks hub.
LINKS: P1-P5 side quests; sets up M12.

## M12 — "Cinder Keep" (CLIMB-GRAPPLE tool; SHARD #2; truth)
GOAL: dungeon; grapple tool; shard #2; the order's complicity.
BEATS: enter the ruined keep -> earn CLIMB-GRAPPLE (verticality, pull objects/enemies) -> grapple
puzzles -> boss (learn-the-pattern, uses grapple + dodge/block) -> SHARD #2 -> records reveal the
ORDER KNEW the flame was a bound god and CHOSE the lie (for order/power).
REWARD: grapple tool, shard #2, heart container, the order's complicity (truth).
LINKS: grapple opens backtrack gates (Greenhollow/Marsh ledges); deepens distrust of Sela's order.

## M13 — "The Tidewreck Coast"
GOAL: reach the storm coast; smuggler/fisher intrigue.
BEATS: east road (grass breaks to dunes/rock) -> Saltbreak harbour; smugglers + fishers at odds ->
rumour of a shard in a sunken vault, reachable only with a tool you'll find there -> meet a smuggler
(T1) and the lonely lighthouse keeper (T3).
CHOICE: early lean toward smugglers or authorities (T1 seed). DEED logged. REWARD: Coast hub.
LINKS: T1-T5; sets up M14; T5 betrayal seeded by earlier deeds.

## M14 — "The Drowned Vault" (HOOKSHOT tool; SHARD #3; truth + betrayal seed)
GOAL: dungeon; hookshot; shard #3; a betrayal in the lie's past.
BEATS: tide-timed entry -> earn HOOKSHOT (cross gaps, yank enemies/objects) -> hookshot puzzles ->
boss (hookshot-dependent pattern) -> SHARD #3 -> TRUTH: a past oracle BETRAYED their own to hide a
shard here (mirrors Sela's possible betrayal).
REWARD: hookshot, shard #3, heart container, the betrayal-history (truth).
LINKS: hookshot backtrack unlocks; foreshadows M17 Sela betrayal.

## M15 — "The Emberwood"
GOAL: reach the fire/frost wood; the god's fever manifest.
BEATS: south road (leaves turn ash + frost) -> a settlement CAUGHT between a burning half and a frozen
half (E2) -> the wood itself wars with itself -> the way to the heart needs fire/frost.
CHOICE: begin the E2 settlement dilemma (save burn-side or freeze-side). DEED logged (heavy).
REWARD: Emberwood hub. LINKS: E1-E5; sets up M16; the Weeping Tree (E4) deepens the god's pain.

## M16 — "Ember Hollow" (FIRE/FROST tool; SHARD #4; hard mercy/power choice)
GOAL: dungeon; fire/frost tool+spell; shard #4; the god's agony; a weighty choice.
BEATS: enter -> earn FIRE/FROST (burn/freeze gates, elemental combat, spells 1-2) -> elemental
puzzles -> ELEMENTAL BOSS (alternates fire/frost; a twist mid-fight) -> at the shard, you sense the
god's AGONY directly.
CHOICE (heavy, Purity): show MERCY to the god's bound fragment (PURE, weakens you short-term) / seize
its POWER (CORRUPT, strong reward, dark) / take the shard plainly (neutral).
REWARD: fire/frost tool+spells, shard #4, heart container; the full horror of the binding (truth).
DEED logged (major — Purity, ending-gating). LINKS: directly weights the Liberator/Tyrant gating.

## M17 — "Sela's Design" (the betrayal point; trust decision)
GOAL: Sela's true plan revealed; possible betrayal; choose who to trust into the Spire.
BEATS: return toward Greenhollow/Sela with 4 shards -> Sela reveals her PLAN for the shards ->
depending on DEEDS/KARMA + M10 choice, Sela either (a) is honest and aligned, (b) BETRAYS you (tries
to take the shards — the seeded betrayal; if you reported Hagga in M10 / leaned corrupt, it bites
hardest), or (c) you preempt her. Hagga (if allied) warns you.
CHOICE: trust Sela / side with Hagga / go alone. Sets the Spire approach + ending availability.
DIALOGUE: Sela's mask slips (or doesn't) — pays off her "too composed" seeding.
REWARD: the path to the Spire opens; allies/enemies locked for endgame. DEED logged (major).
LINKS: the rare, earned betrayal; gates which endings remain reachable.

================================================================
# ACT 3 — THE SPIRE (M18-M20)
================================================================

## M18 — "The Long Ascent"
GOAL: climb the Spire; a gauntlet using ALL tools; earlier enemies return harder.
BEATS: the ascent — lantern/grapple/hookshot/fire-frost gates in sequence -> harder versions of each
region's archetypes -> DEED-MEMORY echoes (S1): spared enemies/allies appear to help; betrayed ones
hinder; the kicked chicken (!) may cameo. Optional S2 confessions unlock ending nuance.
CHOICE: minor mercy/cruelty beats that still log. REWARD: reach the summit chamber.
LINKS: pays off every tool + many deeds; ramps to M19.

## M19 — "The Binding Chamber" (the over-built-up brutal boss; SHARD #5)
GOAL: the full truth; the hyped brutal boss; the final shard.
BEATS: the binding chamber — the god revealed in full (suffering, vast) -> THE OVER-BUILT-UP BOSS
(hyped all game; genuinely hard; learn-the-pattern + a twist + needs multiple tools) -> claim SHARD #5
-> the god/oracle truth laid bare.
REWARD: shard #5 (all 5); the stage for the choice. LINKS: directly into M20.

## M20 — "The Choice" (THREE ENDINGS)
GOAL: decide the god's fate; the ending branches by whole-game Morality + Purity + Deeds.
BEATS: with all 5 shards, at the bound god, you choose:
- WARDEN (re-bind): available to most; land lives, god suffers on, you carry the weight. Bittersweet.
- TYRANT (seize): gated CRUEL+CORRUPT; you become the new tyrant-power; dark, varies by monstrousness.
- LIBERATOR (free it): gated PURE + truths-gathered + mercy-shown (M16) + Hagga-allied (M10); best/
  secret/hardest; bittersweet-transcendent.
~A DOZEN VARIATIONS layered by deed-memory (epilogue): Bram honored/forgotten; chicken callback;
spared/betrayed allies' fates; promises kept; settlement choice (E2); smuggler/miner factions; Sela's
fate. Each playthrough = one ending; replay from start for others.
DIALOGUE: the god's voice; the weight of every choice. REWARD: the ending + epilogue cards.
LINKS: closes every major thread; the deed-memory payoff is the emotional finale.
