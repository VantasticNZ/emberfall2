# EMBERFALL — THE COMPLETE WORLD BIBLE (the whole game, mapped and peopled)

> The full game thought out as ONE cohesive system: the map, every region, every settlement,
> every named character, the buildings, the explorable areas, how the story threads through the
> geography, and what gates what. This is the "know everything that's needed and how it all fits"
> master plan. Companion to MASTER-DESIGN (systems) + DEPTH-AND-EXCITEMENT (quality bar).
> NOTE: regional casts/quests below are the PLAN - flesh out as each region is built. Greenhollow
> + Region 1 are the v1 vertical slice; Regions 2-5 are the expansion.

================================================================
## PART 1 — THE WORLD MAP (geography as story)
================================================================
Hub-and-spoke. Greenhollow at the heart; five regions radiate out; the Hollow Spire at the far
centre is the endgame, visible from everywhere as the goal on the horizon.

```
                 [HOLLOW SPIRE]            (N, far - endgame, visible from everywhere)
                      |
        [SUNDERED PEAKS]                   (N - mountains, strength)
              |
[ASHEN MARSH] --- [GREENHOLLOW] --- [TIDEWRECK COAST]   (W hub E)
              |
        [EMBERWOOD]                         (S - burning forest, fire/frost)
```

- GREENHOLLOW (centre): home village + hub. Act 1 here; return point all game.
- ASHEN MARSH (W): bog/witch culture. Tool: LANTERN. Truth: the first lie.
- SUNDERED PEAKS (N): mountain/miner culture. Tool: STRENGTH (move boulders). Truth: a shard taken by force.
- TIDEWRECK COAST (E): sea/fisher culture. Tool: HOOKSHOT/GRAPPLE. Truth: the oracle who refused to lie.
- EMBERWOOD (S): burning forest. Tool: FIRE-WARD/FROST. Truth: the Ember's voice.
- HOLLOW SPIRE (N far): the oracle seat. Final dungeon. Truth: the whole lie. The 3 endings.

CONNECTIVITY: each region links to Greenhollow by a path gated (story + tool). Shortcuts/
fast-travel shrines unlock as you progress so the hub stays central without tedium. Some region-
to-region links open late (the world "folds together" - Metroidvania).

RECOMMENDED BUILD ORDER (per ROADMAP): Greenhollow (done) -> ASHEN MARSH as Region 1 (the lantern
is the most universally useful first tool; marsh = atmospheric, gentle difficulty, the "first lie"
is the perfect first truth) -> then Peaks/Coast/Emberwood -> Spire.

## PART 2 — GREENHOLLOW (the hub, mostly built)
================================================================
THEME: warm farming village, the heart you fight for. Gentle. Act 1 home; post-catastrophe hub.
AREAS: the village proper (buildings below), the Outskirts (meadow + woods edge + boarded cave),
later a rebuilt/scarred adult version.
BUILDINGS + WHO:
- HOME (hero's): family. Act 1 start.
- MARA'S (bakery): Mara - warm baker, mother-figure. Bread quest (Act 1).
- SMITHY: Hodge - gruff smith. Forges your first WOODEN SWORD (Act 2 earn-weapon). Hammer quest.
- SHOP: shopkeeper (name TBD - e.g. Pell) - buy/sell, the economy hub.
- TAVERN: Bryn - crude, funny old tavernkeep. Gossip = rumours/quest hooks. Tone anchor.
- BARN: farm animals incl. Henrietta the hen (the Chicken childhood choice).
- EDDA'S: Old Edda - elderly herbalist/wise-woman. Healing, lore, the Herb Sprig.
- CHAPEL: the oracle's local shrine - foreshadows the Hearthflame lie. A priest/acolyte NPC.
CHILD FRIENDS (Act 1): Pem, Tam, Nessa - playmates; seed the childhood choices + later callbacks
(they grow up too; some survive the catastrophe, some don't - emotional web).
KEY NPC: BRAM - the hero's guardian/protector. Lost in the catastrophe (his fate = a mystery
thread). The emotional core of Act 1.
STORY ROLE: establish what's worth saving; seed the 3 childhood choices (Chicken/Coin/Secret);
the catastrophe; Sela arrives to send you on the quest.

## PART 3 — THE FIVE REGIONS (each fully specced)
================================================================
Each region template: THEME / AREAS / SETTLEMENT + NPCs / DUNGEON / TOOL / BOSS / SHARD / TRUTH /
LOCAL STORY / KARMA HOOKS / SECRETS. (v1 builds Region 1 fully; rest are the plan.)

### REGION 1 — THE ASHEN MARSH (W) — the LANTERN, the first lie
- THEME: misty bog, drowned ruins, witch-light, half-truths. Atmospheric, melancholy, a little
  spooky but not cruel. Gentle first-region difficulty.
- AREAS: the marsh paths (easy to get turned around - the lantern helps), a drowned ruin, the
  witch's hut, a sunken shrine (dungeon).
- SETTLEMENT: Mirefen - a small stilt-village of bog-folk. NPCs: a wary elder, a marsh-guide, a
  child who knows a secret path, a fisher of strange things. + THE WITCH (Hagga? - an EXILED
  ORACLE, the local mystery; not evil - she fled the lie).
- DUNGEON: the Sunken Shrine - core mechanic LANTERN (light dark rooms, reveal hidden paths/
  switches, ward off shadow-things). Lock-and-key + light puzzles.
- TOOL: THE LANTERN - lights darkness; retroactively reveals hidden things across the whole world
  (incl. a dark corner of Greenhollow's cave). The first big "see the world anew" tool.
- BOSS: a shadow/drowned guardian - tests using light to expose its weak point.
- SHARD: Ember Shard #1. TRUTH: the first crack in the oracle lie - the witch reveals the
  Hearthflame isn't just a fire (she was exiled for saying so).
- LOCAL STORY: the bog-folk fear the witch; the truth is she's protecting them / was wronged by
  the oracles. A karma choice: expose/defend/exploit her.
- KARMA HOOKS: help vs exploit the bog-folk; believe vs report the witch; mercy to the shadow-things.
- SECRETS: lantern-revealed caches, a heart piece in the drowned ruin, an optional sad NPC story.

### REGION 2 — THE SUNDERED PEAKS (N) — STRENGTH, a shard taken by force
- THEME: harsh mountains, mining town, rockfalls, old wounds. Tougher.
- AREAS: switchback trails, a mine, a cliff shrine (dungeon), an avalanche-blocked pass.
- SETTLEMENT: Karthspan - a mining town. NPCs: a hard mine-boss, a union of weary miners, a
  blacksmith (better gear), a kid who wants out. A RIVAL SHARD-SEEKER first appears here.
- DUNGEON: the Deepdelve - mechanic STRENGTH (move boulders, smash rock, push blocks). 
- TOOL: STRENGTH (gauntlets/might) - move boulders in the world (opens many old gates).
- BOSS: a rock golem / the mine's guardian - strength + timing.
- SHARD #2. TRUTH: a shard was TAKEN BY FORCE generations ago - the oracles' violence. The
  miners' curse is shard-corruption.
- LOCAL STORY: the miners are cursed/sick from digging near the shard; free them or exploit the
  mine. The rival seeker complicates it.
- KARMA: side with miners vs boss; mercy vs ruthlessness with the rival.
- SECRETS: strength-gated caches, a heart piece behind a boulder, a hidden veteran miner's tale.

### REGION 3 — THE TIDEWRECK COAST (E) — HOOKSHOT, the oracle who refused
- THEME: storm coast, shipwrecks, fishers, a lighthouse. Open, windy, bittersweet.
- AREAS: cliffs + beaches, a wreck-field, a lighthouse, a tidal shrine (dungeon).
- SETTLEMENT: Saltmere - a fishing port. NPCs: a storm-worn harbourmaster, a smuggler (Corrupt-
  gated content), a lighthouse keeper who KNOWS things, a widow of a lost oracle.
- DUNGEON: the Tidal Shrine - mechanic HOOKSHOT/GRAPPLE (cross gaps, pull to ledges, grab switches).
- TOOL: HOOKSHOT - traversal + combat + puzzle; reaches the unreachable.
- BOSS: a sea-guardian / a great eel - grapple to expose it.
- SHARD #3. TRUTH: an ORACLE WHO REFUSED TO LIE - exiled/drowned here. Her records survive in the
  lighthouse. The lie has dissenters; the player learns the oracles are not united.
- LOCAL STORY: the smuggler vs harbourmaster; the widow's quest to clear her oracle's name.
- KARMA: smuggling (Corrupt) vs honest work; truth vs silence about the dead oracle.
- SECRETS: grapple-only islets, a heart piece on a sea-stack, a message in a bottle thread.

### REGION 4 — THE EMBERWOOD (S) — FIRE-WARD/FROST, the Ember's voice
- THEME: a forest perpetually, unnaturally burning - beautiful and wrong. The Ember is near.
- AREAS: burning groves, ash-clearings, a frozen counterpoint, the Heartfire shrine (dungeon).
- SETTLEMENT: Cinderhold - folk who live WITH the fire (fire-cultists? survivors?). NPCs: a
  fire-priest (zealot - believes the lie hardest), a defector, a child immune to the flame
  (significant), a hermit who hears the Ember too.
- DUNGEON: the Heartfire Shrine - mechanic FIRE-WARD / FROST (pass fire/thorns, freeze to cross,
  fire/ice state puzzles).
- TOOL: FIRE-WARD (+ FROST) - elemental traversal + combat (the magic rung of the weapon ladder).
- BOSS: a fire-elemental / a guardian made of the Ember's pain.
- SHARD #4. TRUTH: the hero HEARS THE EMBER'S VOICE - confirmation it's a suffering, bound god.
  The mid/late game turn: the player can no longer unknow it.
- LOCAL STORY: the cult worships the lie; a defector wants the truth; the immune child is key to
  the Liberator path.
- KARMA: zealotry vs mercy; feed the cult vs free minds; how you treat the suffering Ember.
- SECRETS: fire-gated groves, a heart piece in the frozen heart, the hermit's whole sad arc.

### REGION 5 — THE HOLLOW SPIRE (N, far) — the final key, the whole lie, the endings
- THEME: the oracle seat - cold marble, hidden machinery, the bound Ember beneath. Climax.
- AREAS: the ascent, the oracle halls, the binding chamber (final dungeon).
- SETTLEMENT: the oracle order itself (not a friendly town - the antagonist seat). SELA's true
  role revealed here. The HIGH ORACLE (final antagonist? or tragic figure?).
- DUNGEON: the Spire - uses ALL tools (the mastery test); the binding-chamber puzzle.
- BOSS: the High Oracle / the Ember's bound form / a choice-driven final confrontation.
- SHARD #5 + ALL CONVERGE. TRUTH: the WHOLE lie - what the Hearthflame is, what binding it costs,
  what freeing it risks. Sela's part in it.
- THE ENDINGS branch here (per MASTER-DESIGN): WARDEN (re-bind, bittersweet), TYRANT (seize,
  Cruel+Corrupt), LIBERATOR (free it, Pure+truth+mercy - the immune child, the defectors, the
  refusing oracle all pay off here).

## PART 4 — THE GATING MAP (what blocks what - the Metroidvania spine)
================================================================
- Greenhollow cave/Outskirts -> opens as ADULT (story) -> leads toward Region 1.
- ASHEN MARSH: entered after the catastrophe/Sela's quest begins. LANTERN earned here ->
  retroactively opens: dark cave corners (Greenhollow + everywhere), Marsh dark paths.
- SUNDERED PEAKS: gated until you have the LANTERN (dark mine sections) + story flag. STRENGTH
  earned here -> opens: boulders everywhere (back in Marsh, Greenhollow, Coast).
- TIDEWRECK COAST: gated by a chasm/water needing HOOKSHOT-adjacent or story; HOOKSHOT earned ->
  opens: gaps + grapple points world-wide.
- EMBERWOOD: gated by fire/thorns needing FIRE-WARD; earned -> opens: fire/ice gates world-wide.
- HOLLOW SPIRE: gated until ALL 5 shards (or 4 + story) - the final ascent.
- SEEDED EARLY: bombable cracks (from Act 1), dark corners, boulders, gaps, fire - all VISIBLE
  before you can pass them, so "come back later" threads exist from the start (telegraph!).

## PART 7 — HOW IT ALL FITS (the one-paragraph synthesis)
================================================================
You grow up in Greenhollow (learn to care), lose it (the catastrophe), and Sela sends you to
gather 5 Ember Shards across 5 regions. Each region gives a TOOL (that opens the world further,
Metroidvania-style), a SHARD, a BOSS, and a TRUTH that cracks the oracle lie a little more - while
its LOCAL story + KARMA choices shape who you become and who'll deal with you. The dissenters you
meet (the exiled witch, the refusing oracle, the defector, the immune child) compound into the
knowledge + means to free the Ember. At the Hollow Spire all shards + truths converge, and WHO
YOU'VE BECOME (karma + choices + truths uncovered) decides which ending you can reach: re-bind the
god (Warden), seize it (Tyrant), or free it (Liberator). Every area carries the spine + a local
story + the personal arc; every tool opens new exploration; every choice echoes. That's the whole
cohesive game.
